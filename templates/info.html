Welcome to this new active learning experiment!

Information

The categorization task is based on a rule that you will have to guess based on the examples that will be shown to you. The experiment is divided in three parts.

  • Initially, you will be shown some of the images and their associated categories, marked by either 0 or 1.

  • Then, you will be asked to categorize some new images. You will receive feedback on whether or not you chose the right category for each image.

    • During this first phase, you should try to infer the rule based on the feedback you receive.

  • The last step is the testing phase, you will again be asked to categorize some images but feedback won't be provided.

    • Data Privacy

    No user-related information is collected, the record of the experiment is stored in a private server. If you have any questions about the research/data/other, you can contact florence.robert-regol[at]mail.mcgill.ca

    By clicking on 'Start the experiment', you agree to participate in the experiment and consent to the recording and storage of your data as part of this experiment.
